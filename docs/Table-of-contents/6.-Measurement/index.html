    Measurement #

    The four qubits are then measured in a fixed but arbitray basis. Arbitrary since you can always rotate into a different basis, in this case this rotation is just leaned and not explicitly applied.

    The process of preparing quantum states, embedding data, applying rotations according to the parametervecor theta and measureing the outcome are performed muliple times.

    dev = qml.device("default.qubit", wires=4, shots=1000)

    This is referred to as “shots” and will lead to a probability distribution over the possible states.
